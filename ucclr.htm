UC Committee on Latino Research (UCCLR)

The University of California Committee on Latino Research (UCCLR) advises the President on the use and distribution of research funds dedicated to the promotion and dissemination of knowledge on Chicano and Latino issues of policy relevance to California, and oversees a program of competitive funding in research infrastructure support on the UC campuses.
